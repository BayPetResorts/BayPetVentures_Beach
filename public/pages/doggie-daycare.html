Doggie Daycare

The All-Inclusive Package

  • We offer free pickup and drop-off service in the South Bay area. Your dog will be safely transported to and from our facility, making it convenient for you and stress-free for your pet.

  • All adventures are led by a CCPDT-Certified Trainer, ensuring your dog receives professional guidance and training throughout the day. Our certified trainers use positive reinforcement techniques to help your dog learn and grow.

  • Dogs get along much better in open areas like the beach where there are far fewer territorial disputes and more stimulation. The natural environment provides endless opportunities for exploration, play, and positive social interactions with other dogs.

  • After a fun day at the beach, your dog will receive a thorough refresh using Tek Shampoo (originally developed for NASA research) for the best deep clean, and Virbac Epi-Otic Advanced Ear Cleanser to refresh and protect your dog's ears after a day of beach activities.

  • We take tons of videos and photos throughout the day, capturing all the special moments of your dog's adventure. You'll receive a comprehensive report card with photos and videos showing your dog's activities, interactions, and fun moments.
