Our Services

Everything you need to know about what we offer for your dog's beach adventure.

Door-to-Door Pickup & Drop-Off

We handle all transportation so you don't have to. Your dog rides in a climate-controlled back seat set up for comfort and security with an extended platform (similar to a RuffRover-style setup). Pickup and drop-off is typically at your door, and times are scheduled directly with you.

Service Area: South Bay pickups and drop-offs

Certified Supervised Session

Outings are run by Charles, a certified trainer and experienced pet sitter with 20,000+ hours working with dogs. Each session includes supervised playdate on either a quieter beach or a more social beach. On request, we can focus on recall and anxiety reduction, designed to support confidence, focus, and healthy social exposure.

Perfect for: Anxious dogs who are learning to socialize. We may do an initial consultation to see if the dog is trainable and determine the best approach.

Beach Adventure & Socialization

The 4-hour timer starts when we pick up your dog. We drive to Santa Cruz West Cliff dog beach (or Sunny Cove for private reservations when appropriate), where we spend about 4 hours engaging in walks, play, and structured calm time.

Private Outings: One dog per outing to ensure individualized attention and maximum safety.

Luxury Treats Snack Break

During the adventure, your dog enjoys premium luxury treats as part of their snack break. We offer high-quality options to make their experience extra special.

Current Treat Options:
  • Bonne Vie French Cheese Pyramid (Goat Cheese Cubes)
  • Vital Essentials Freeze-Dried Treats Minnows

Treat options can be swapped out upon request. Please let us know if your dog has any dietary restrictions or preferences.

Bath, Brush & Shampoo Refresh

Your dog comes home clean and refreshed. We use premium grooming products to ensure your pup looks and feels great after their adventure. If your dog has specific skin sensitivities or allergies, please let us know and we'll accommodate.

Grooming Products We Use:
  • Micro-Tek Shampoo
  • Grooming Brush

Grooming products can be swapped with personal products upon request. Please let us know if your dog has any skin sensitivities or product preferences.

Photo/Video Report Card

We love taking lots of pictures and videos of the dogs! You'll receive a photo and video report card documenting your dog's adventure, so you can see all the fun they had. Feel free to ask for more photos or videos anytime.
